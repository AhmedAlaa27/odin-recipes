Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Salt and Vinegar

Steps

  1. Bring the salt, vinegar, and water to a boil.
  2. Gently boil the eggs for about 14 minutes.
  3. Transfer the eggs to ice water.
  4. Once cooled, peel the eggs.
